Tips to Identify Fake Reviews

Become a more informed and confident online shopper.

How to Spot Fake Reviews

  • Exaggerated Praise: Be cautious of reviews using phrases like "best ever" or "absolutely perfect" without context.
  • Reviewer Activity: Check if the reviewer has only one review or has a suspicious profile.
  • Repetitive Wording: Watch for repeated phrases across multiple reviews, which may indicate copy-pasting.
  • Vague Feedback: Avoid reviews that are overly emotional but provide no specific product details.
  • Use Detection Tools: Leverage AI-powered platforms—like this one—to detect patterns of authenticity.
